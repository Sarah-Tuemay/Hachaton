Network Administrator

Position: Network Administrator

Location: Campus Network Center

Duration: 6 months

Salary: Competitive, based on experience


Expectation

We are seeking a skilled Network Administrator to join our team at the Campus Network Center. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing and maintaining our network infrastructure, ensuring optimal performance and security.


Responsibilities

  • Monitor network performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • Configure and maintain network devices (routers, switches, firewalls).
  • Implement security measures to protect the network.
  • Collaborate with IT teams to support network-related projects.
  • Document network configurations and changes.
  • Provide technical support to end-users.
  • Stay updated with the latest networking technologies.

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a Computer Science or related program.
  • Strong understanding of networking concepts and protocols.
  • Experience with network devices and configurations.
  • Knowledge of network security practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
  • Strong communication skills.

work schedule

Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM


Application Process

If you are interested in this position, please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your relevant experience and skills.
